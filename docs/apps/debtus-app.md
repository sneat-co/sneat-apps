# debtus-app — Debts & Debtors

> **https://debtus.app** · `apps/debtus-app`

A focused mini-app for tracking personal debts — who owes you money, and whom you owe.

## Summary

Keep a clear record of informal loans and debts so nothing gets forgotten or awkward.

## Home Page

The debtus-app home page is the starting page of the **Debts module** in [sneat-app](sneat-app.md).
